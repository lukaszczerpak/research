
WYMAGANIA
=================================

W ~/.m2/settings.xml zdefiniować properties:

        <glassfish.remote.hostname>192.168.1.11</glassfish.remote.hostname>
        <glassfish.remote.username>admin</glassfish.remote.username>
        <glassfish.remote.password>admin</glassfish.remote.password>

np. w profilu aktywowanym zmienną (mvn -Dgf=remoting clean cargo:deploy test):

        <profile>
            <id>glassfish-remoting</id>
            <activation>
                <activeByDefault>false</activeByDefault>
            </activation>
            <properties>
                <glassfish.remote.hostname>192.168.1.11</glassfish.remote.hostname>
                <glassfish.remote.username>admin</glassfish.remote.username>
                <glassfish.remote.password>admin</glassfish.remote.password>
            </properties>
        </profile>

DEPLOY
=================================

mvn clean package cargo:deploy -DskipTests -P glassfish-remoting
